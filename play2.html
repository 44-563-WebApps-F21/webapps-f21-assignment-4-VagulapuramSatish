MRS. ALLONBY.

Ah, my husband is a sort of promissory note; I’m tired of meeting him.

LADY CAROLINE.

But you renew him from time to time, don’t you?

MRS. ALLONBY.
Oh no, Lady Caroline. I have only had one husband as yet.

I suppose you look upon me as quite an amateur.

LADY CAROLINE.

With your views on life I wonder you married at all.

MRS. ALLONBY.

So do I.

LADY HUNSTANTON.

life, but that you like to hide your happiness from others.

MRS. ALLONBY.

I assure you I was horribly deceived in Ernest.
